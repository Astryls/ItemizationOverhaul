Runewords

Sixteen recipes - socket the exact runes in the exact order to forge a Mythic runeword.

All 16 runewords, four per slot. Carve the runes into a non-set item of the right slot in this exact order, filling every socket, and the item becomes a Mythic with these bonuses on top of the individual runes. See Runewords for the rules.
