Ethereal, Indestructible & Replenishing

Two special modifiers that roll independently of the affix budget - plus the enchantment that stackable gear rolls instead of ordinary affixes.

Ethereal

An Ethereal item is translucent and supercharged. It grants +50% to its real base stats - armor, insulation, and damage - on top of whatever affixes it rolled. The trade-off in classic ARPG fashion: it cannot be repaired.

  • The +50% applies through the same stat pipeline as base-stat variance, so it stacks with the item's own ±15% roll.
  • Ethereal also boosts every affix value by 50% when the item is rolled, Greater affixes included (Artifact values keep their fixed over-cap numbers).
  • The item renders at reduced opacity (tunable in settings; default 50%).

Indestructible

An Indestructible item never loses hit points and never deteriorates - it will outlast your colony. White items never roll it. It is damage-immune, not disposal-immune: you can still smelt or destroy it on purpose, and the cube can grant it deliberately.

Roll odds

Both modifiers roll on an independent chance, scaled by tier, separate from the affix count. They are mutually exclusive - if both land, one is dropped at random.

Stackable gear & the Replenishing enchant

Gear that stacks - throwing weapons and similar consumable equipment - plays by its own rule: it never rolls ordinary per-item affixes, because independent rolls could not survive stack merging. Instead, a stackable drop has a 7% chance to spawn as a proper enchanted stack:

  • It rolls a real tier (Magic, Rare, or Legendary) with that tier's normal affixes and a Diablo name.
  • A bonus Replenishing affix is added: the stack slowly restores its own quantity toward its stack limit while it sits in storage (not while equipped or carried).
  • Different rolls remain separate stacks, exactly like items of different quality, and splitting a stack keeps the enchantment on both halves.
Cube-legal. Rare and better Replenishing stacks are valid Luminary Cube gear: reroll or upgrade them under the usual tier rules. Rerolling can replace the Replenishing affix itself, so gamble deliberately.
Migration. On older saves, previously-affixed stackables without the Replenishing enchant are quietly returned to plain - the per-instance rolls they carried were the source of stack-merge bugs.

At the Luminary Cube

You can make any non-ethereal item Indestructible at the Luminary Cube. Ethereal arrives naturally on rolls and through the Spectral blessing, which also overcharges affixes uncapped.
