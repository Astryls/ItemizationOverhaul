Delving & Floors

The descent loop in detail - the entrance, the stairs, depth scaling, and getting your party (and loot) home.

Getting in

Unlock the Catacomb Delving research to learn how to build a reinforced stairwell that opens a stable passage into the deep rock. Construct the catacombs entrance and order colonists down - they arrive on the first generated floor with a letter warning that the guardians strengthen as they descend.

The stairs

ObjectFunction
Catacombs entranceThe surface stairwell; the way in and the anchor for return portals.
Descending stairsFound on each floor - take them to generate and drop into the next, deeper floor.
Ascending stairsClimb back toward the surface, one floor at a time.
Town portalSnap the party home; a paired portal opens at your entrance to return to that depth.

Depth scaling

Every floor down raises the stakes on two axes at once:

DangerGuardians grow stronger and more numerous - insect hives early, Anomaly entities deeper.
RewardSarcophagi and chests roll better Core-tier loot the deeper you crack them.
DressingThe crypt's stone and fittings shift with depth, so the floor itself shows how far you have come.

This is the classic ARPG risk-reward curve: stop at a comfortable depth and farm it, or push deeper for richer loot against deadlier guardians.

Cracking sarcophagi

A sealed sarcophagus is heavy with grave-goods. Order a colonist to crack it open and claim what is inside - the deeper the floor, the richer the contents. Expect unidentified gear, runes, and the occasional relic among the bones.

Getting home with the haul

The town portal is the key to a sustainable delve. Push as deep as you dare, then open a portal: the party and everything they carry snap to the surface, and a matching portal at your entrance lets you drop straight back to that depth next time - no re-clearing the floors above.

Worked example. Your party reaches floor 8 and the Anomaly guardians are getting hairy. Rather than risk the climb, they crack one last sarcophagus, open a town portal, and step home with full packs. Back at base they identify the loot, heal up, restock potions, and return to floor 8 through the paired surface portal to make their run at floor 9.
Plan your exit. The deeper you are, the longer the climb back - carry Town Portal scrolls (Scrolls & Potions) as a backup extraction even if you have not built a portal yet.
