Named, fixed-identity legends - 34 Uniques that can manifest on any gear of the right slot, and 14 Mythic artifacts of impossible power.

Uniques - 34 legends, every slot

When gear rolls Relic tier, it has a chance (default 25%, tunable) to manifest as a named Unique. Uniques are bound to slot types - any helmet, any outer armor, any melee weapon - so gear from other mods can spawn as a Unique too.

  • Always identical - fixed name, fixed affixes at full Artifact ✦ potency, base variance pinned. Two copies of The Forefather are the same blade.
  • RimWorld retellings of Diablo legends - the Harlequin Crest becomes the Crest of the Laughing Court; Windforce becomes the Windreaper.
  • Seven slot families - headgear, outer armor, clothing, legwear, utility, melee, ranged.
  • A gold ✦ Unique ✦ ground tag replaces the standard Relic tag.

See the Unique Compendium for all 34, each with its exact affixes and lore.

Mythics - beyond the rules

Above the Uniques sit 14 Mythics - affixes overcharged 25% beyond Artifact, plus a one-of-a-kind Mythic Power no other item can grant. Mythics never drop from the world; they are earned. See the Mythics page.

The expedition chains

A Mythic is won at the end of a multi-leg expedition chain - a trail of escalating ruins, each yielding a star-map fragment that points to the next. See Expedition Chains.

Odyssey integration

With the Odyssey DLC active, every Odyssey unique weapon reward manifests as one of our named Uniques - relic tier, fixed loadout, full lore - while keeping Odyssey's custom art and ability slot. No more random trait soup; every reward is a legend with a name.

Hunted relics

Some Uniques and Mythics surface as ideology relics another faction claims - registered on their ideoligion page under the item's true name. Owning one makes that faction hostile: reclaim raids at home, and ambushes on the world map while a caravan carries it. (See Relics & Grudges for the shared grudge system.)

Compatibility. Slot classification reads layers and body groups at runtime, so modded gear qualifies automatically. The mythic glow follows weapons into hands and holsters (Show Me Your Hands / Holsters compatible). Junk - logs, beer, single-use launchers - is excluded from both systems.
