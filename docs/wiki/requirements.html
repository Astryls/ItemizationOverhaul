Requirement Model

The exact, deterministic scoring that turns an item's roll into a level and stat demand.

Power Score

Item power
base = { White:0, Rare:8, Legendary:20, Relic:32 } power = base + 1 per affix + 2 extra per Greater affix + 4 extra per Artifact affix + (Corrupted ? 4 : 0) + (Cleansed ? 6 : 0) + (FactionRelic ? 6 : 0) + max(0, quality - Normal) power = max(1, power)

The required level equals the Power Score. White / plain items score 0 and demand nothing.

Stat demands

High-power items also demand attributes. The primary stat is keyed to the item's role; a secondary stat kicks in once the item is Legendary-class (power ≥ 20).

Stat requirements
primary = 5 + ceil(power · 0.75) secondary = 5 + ceil(power · 0.40) # only when power ≥ 20
Item typePrimary → Secondary
Ranged weaponDexterity → Strength
Melee weaponStrength → Dexterity
ApparelVitality → Strength
JewelryWisdom → Charisma

Special cases

  • Uniques are priced at a flat 48, Mythics at 60 (modifiers still stack), and Mythics add a tertiary Wisdom demand - "Mythic Attunement," because a god-forged artifact needs a mind strong enough to hold it.
  • Runes: a rune's level floor is ceil(ladderIndex × 1.2); a socketed item's level is max(item, highest rune) - Diablo 2 rules, never additive.
  • Jewelry: worn pieces use tier base plus rung bonuses (Lesser +1 … Divine +7), Sanctified +6, Desecrated -6; charms are level-only and gate at attunement rather than equip.
Worked example. A corrupted Legendary greatsword with 5 affixes, two of them Greater, scores 20 + 5 + 2×2 + 4(corrupt) = 33. It requires Level 33, Strength 5 + ceil(33×0.75) = 30, and - being power ≥ 20 - Dexterity 5 + ceil(33×0.40) = 19. A genuine endgame blade.

Enforcement

A colonist who doesn't meet an item's level and stat demands simply cannot equip or wear it — the attempt is blocked with a reason. The gate sits on the equip path itself, so it holds no matter how the item is equipped:

  • Vanilla equip and wear orders.
  • True RPG Inventory — the drag-to-slot and float-menu Wear / Equip actions are gated at the source, before any inventory state changes.
  • Simple Sidearms — taking a sidearm into a weapon slot is checked the same way.
Nothing is stored. The score is recomputed on demand, so re-rolling or cleansing an item instantly updates its requirement.
