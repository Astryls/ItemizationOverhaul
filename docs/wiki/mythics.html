Mythics

Fourteen artifacts overcharged beyond Artifact tier, each with a power no other item can grant.

All 14 Mythics — affixes overcharged beyond Artifact, each with a one-of-a-kind Mythic Power. Won only at the end of an expedition chain, never dropped. ★ = Greater, ✦ = Artifact.

Like Uniques, every Mythic carries its own fixed artwork - its name as the title and its lore below as the description, in the vanilla Art tab.
