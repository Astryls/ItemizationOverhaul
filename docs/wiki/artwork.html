Item Artwork

The rarest items become genuine RimWorld artworks - a title, a tale-based description, an "Author" line, and the vanilla Art tab - just like a masterwork sculpture.

What it is

Every affixed weapon and apparel quietly carries the vanilla art component. It stays dormant on ordinary gear and only wakes up on the three top identities - Relic, Unique, and Mythic. When it does, the item gains everything vanilla art has: a generated title, a "this depicts..." description, an author, and a dedicated Art tab in the inspect pane. The text is captured once and stored on the item, so it never drifts between saves.

Mythicthe Mythic's own nameits fixed, authored lore

Uniques and Mythics use fixed, always-identical art - every copy of a legend reads the same. Relics are procedural, so their art is generated.

How relic art is generated

Relic art leans on RimWorld's own art grammar for the funny, evocative part, then adds one line of its own so the piece still nods to the item's rolls:

  • Title & body - drawn from the vanilla art rule-packs, chosen to fit the item type: melee weapons, guns, and apparel each use their matching grammar. This is the same machinery that names and describes masterwork sculptures, so you get the familiar RimWorld humour.
  • Affix motif line - a short sentence appended to the description, built from the item's strongest affix (a relic power, then an aura, then a marquee stat). A fiery aura reads as smouldering imagery; heavy armor as iron and bulwarks; and so on.
  • Author - a faction relic credits the owning faction; every other relic credits "the Ancients". A relic you forged yourself keeps its crafter as the author.
Deterministic. Each relic seeds its own art once and stores it, so the funny description and its motif line stay exactly the same every time you open the item - across reloads too.

Where it shows

  • Art tab - select the item; the vanilla Art tab appears in the inspect pane with the title and full description.
  • Info card - the ⓘ info window includes the art block (title, description, author) alongside the affix stats.

Settings

Under the Artwork tab of Options → Mod settings → Itemization Overhaul - Core:

Enable item artworkmaster toggle for relic, unique and mythic art (default on)
Append affix motif lineadd the affix-derived sentence to relic descriptions (default on)
Why it exists. A named legend deserves to read like one. Borrowing RimWorld's art system means relics feel native to the game - a found artifact with a story - while uniques and mythics get their authored lore presented in the same familiar frame.
