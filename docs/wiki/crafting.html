Crafting & Config

How a master smith forges better loot, the settings that shape every roll, and how to switch any individual affix on or off.

Skill-scaled crafting - the two-stage roll

When a colonist crafts a weapon or apparel, its rarity is rolled from the crafter's skill in the recipe's work skill, not the world drop table. Crafting resolves in two stages: first an enchant gate decides whether the item is special at all - a failed roll comes out plain - and only if it passes is a tier rolled. So skill controls both how often a crafter produces enchanted gear and how high it can roll.

Crafted odds (by skill)
# stage 1 - is it enchanted at all? (fail = plain) enchant = enchMax · clamp01( (skill - enchMin) / (20 - enchMin) ) # stage 2 - tier of the enchanted item relic = (relicMax>0 && maxTier≥3 && skill≥18) ? min(relicMax, (skill-17)·relicMax/8) : 0 legendary = clamp01( clamp01((skill-8)/12)·legMax + growth·min(0.2,(skill-20)·0.01) ) rare = min( clamp01((skill-4)/16)·0.6 , 1 - legendary ) magic = 1 - legendary - rare # then hard-capped to maxTier

Shipped defaults

Out of the box crafts top out at Rare, the enchant gate opens at skill 10, and a master enchants three crafts in four. Every value below is a slider in the Crafting tab.

enchMax = 75%
Enchant chance at skill 20.
enchMin = 10
Below this skill, crafts never enchant.
maxTier = Rare
Hard cap on the crafted tier - the top tiers stay loot.
legMax = 0%
Legendary ceiling - off by default.
relicMax = 20%
Relic ceiling - dormant while the tier cap is below Legendary.
endless growth = on
Skill above 20 keeps nudging the tier split upward.

Want Legendary crafts? Raise Highest craftable tier to Legendary (or Relic) and lift Max Legendary chance above 0%. With the cap at Relic, a grandmaster of skill 18+ can occasionally forge a pink Relic. The Crafting tab shows a live outcome preview per skill level that updates as you drag.

The per-affix configuration window

Open Options → Mod settings → Itemization Overhaul - Core and click "Configure individual affixes…" to open a searchable window where every affix can be toggled on or off. Disabled affixes never roll, on any item, anywhere.

The settings tabs

Drop rates & tiers
Enchanted item chance (default 20%), tier chances, Relic chance, Greater chance, potency multiplier.
Affixes
Skill affix share, aura chance, tech-aware affix gating, and the per-affix window.
Artwork
Relic / unique / mythic art and the affix motif line.
Visuals
Ethereal opacity (default 50%), aura glow brightness (default 30%), aura particle brightness (default 75%).
Crafting
The crafting curve above, the Horadric grid toggle, and delivery logistics.
Market value
Per-tier value multipliers: Magic ×1.4, Rare ×2, Legendary ×3.5, Relic ×6.
Changes apply to newly-spawned gear; items already rolled keep their affixes.
