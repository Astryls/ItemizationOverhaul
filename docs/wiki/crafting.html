Crafting & Config

How a master smith forges better loot, the settings that shape every roll, and how to switch any individual affix on or off.

Skill-scaled crafting — the two-stage roll

When a colonist crafts a weapon or apparel, its rarity is rolled from the crafter's skill in the recipe's work skill, not the world drop table. Crafting resolves in two stages: first an enchant gate decides whether the item is special at all — a failed roll comes out plain (a mundane vanilla item) — and only if it passes is a tier rolled. So skill controls both how often a crafter produces enchanted gear and how high it can roll.

Crafted odds (by skill)
# stage 1 - is it enchanted at all? (fail = plain White) enchant = enchMax · clamp01( (skill - enchMin) / (20 - enchMin) ) # stage 2 - tier of the enchanted item relic = (relicMax>0 && maxTier≥3 && skill≥18) ? min(relicMax, (skill-17)·relicMax/8) : 0 legendary = clamp01( clamp01((skill-8)/12)·legMax + growth·min(0.2,(skill-20)·0.01) ) rare = min( clamp01((skill-4)/16)·0.6 , 1 - legendary ) magic = 1 - legendary - rare # then hard-capped to maxTier

Shipped defaults

Out of the box the suite ships a deliberately restrained crafting curve: crafts top out at Rare, and even a grandmaster leaves the workshop with a plain item half the time. Every value below is a slider in the Crafting tab.

enchMax = 50%
Enchant chance at skill 20 (scales down with skill).
enchMin = 8
Below this skill, crafts never enchant.
legMax = 0%
Legendary ceiling — off by default.
maxTier = Rare
Hard cap on the crafted tier.
relicMax = 20%
Relic ceiling — dormant while the tier cap is below Legendary.
endless growth = on
Skill above 20 keeps nudging the Legendary share (once uncapped).

Want Legendary crafts back? Raise Highest craftable tier to Legendary and lift Max Legendary chance above 0%. That also wakes the dormant 20% Relic ceiling, so a grandmaster could occasionally forge a pink Relic. The Crafting tab shows a live outcome preview at skill 8 / 14 / 20 that updates as you drag.

Aura brightness (Visuals tab)

The aura glow around a pawn wearing aura gear is split into two independently-scalable layers, both 0–200% (default 100%):

Aura glow brightness
The ground glow disc and the spinning rune beneath the pawn.
Aura particle brightness
The floating orbs and elemental particles (embers, snow, sparks).

Set either to 0% to hide that layer entirely — the effect isn't drawn at all, so it costs nothing.

The per-affix configuration window

Open Options → Mod settings → Itemization Overhaul - Core and click "Configure individual affixes…" to open a searchable window where every affix can be toggled on or off. Disabled affixes never roll, on any item, anywhere.

The window groups affixes into collapsible categories, supports search and per-row descriptions, and has All / None / Reset controls. Addons register their own entries through the same API, so the one window governs the whole suite.

Use it to curate. Hate a particular aura, or want a pure-melee game with no ranged affixes? Switch them off here and they vanish from every future roll without touching anything else.
All settings live under Options → Mod settings → Itemization Overhaul - Core, split across tabs: Drop rates & tiers, Affixes, Artwork, Visuals, Crafting, and Reset. Changes apply to newly-spawned gear; items already rolled keep their affixes.
