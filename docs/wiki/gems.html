Gems

Seven common socketables that sit below the runes — simple, colour-coded, and slot-dependent.

Gems are the entry tier of the socket system. Where runes climb a 30-step power ladder and combine into runewords, gems are flat: one quality each, no ordering, no words. They drop and trade cheaply, and they socket into the exact same slots as runes — so a fresh colony can start socketing long before the first Vorn drops.

One gem, four effects. Like runes, every gem grants a different effect depending on where it is socketed — weapon, helmet, body armor, or any other gear. Plan the gem around the slot.

Stat effects ride the same equipped-gear pipeline as Core affixes, so they stack with affixes, runes, set bonuses and jewelry alike. Diamond is the premium gem — a straight power/defence pick in every slot — while Skull is the utility pick for melee bruisers.

Signature procs

Four gems carry an on-hit proc instead of a stat when socketed into a weapon. Procs are melee-only and share the same on-hit system as the rune procs, so they can be toggled in settings.

Ruby · Burn
12% chance to set the target alight on a melee hit.
Sapphire · Stun
9% chance to briefly stun the target on a melee hit.
Emerald · Poison
Seeps toxic buildup into the target on every melee hit.
Skull · Lifesteal
Heals the wielder for 15% of the melee damage dealt.
Skulls of the dead. A real skull carved from a corpse (a "skull of…") counts as a Skull gem and grants the same effect when socketed — a grim but free source of lifesteal for a melee colony.

Where gems sit

  • Below runes on the ladder. Gems occupy their own band beneath the 30 runes, so a runeword recipe never accidentally consumes one.
  • Permanent once socketed. Same rule as runes — no removal, no swaps. Choose the slot deliberately.
  • No runewords. Gems don't spell words; only runes do. Mix gems and runes freely in the same item's sockets.

Socketing a gem works exactly like socketing a rune — the base float-menu method or, with True RPG Inventory, drag-and-drop. See Sockets & Runes for both.
