The loot model shift

This addon ships a Core tweak that sharpens the rarity curve to the D2 feel:

  • White is always plain - a White, non-relic roll short-circuits to a mundane item with zero affixes, so common loot is genuinely common.
  • Only Legendary, Ancestral, and Relic carry affixes - and those are exactly the tiers that drop unidentified.
  • Tier odds are raised to 22% Legendary / 6% Ancestral so magic items actually appear among the plain whites.
Relationship to the drop rate. This is a different lever from the Core Affix Drop Rate: the drop rate decides enchanted vs plain per item, while this addon decides that White specifically is always plain, concentrating all affixes into the higher tiers that arrive unidentified.

Three sources for every consumable

SourceDetail
CraftPotions at a Drug Lab (research IOSP_Alchemy); scrolls & tomes at a Drug Lab or the Luminary Cube (research IOSP_Scrollcraft).
TradePotions carry the Drugs trade tag; scrolls and tomes are Exotic.
LootAll carry reward tags - tomes show up in mid-frequency reward caches.
Worked example. A trader's stock includes an unidentified plasteel longsword - it could be anything from a weak Legendary to a god-rolled Relic. You buy it blind, walk it home, and read a Scroll of Identify: it reveals as an Ancestral blade with a Greater damage affix and a socket. The gamble of buying unidentified is the whole point.
