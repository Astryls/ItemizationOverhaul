The Luminary Cube

A crafting station that reshapes your loot - re-roll, upgrade, graft, corrupt, make indestructible, and recycle gear into reagents.

The workbench

Build the Luminary Cube and select it to open Cube Gear - a custom UI listing every affixed item on the map (and worn / carried by colonists). Pick an item and the Cube offers a row of operations, each consuming reagents recycled from unwanted gear.

Operations

OperationWhat it does
Re-rollGambles one affix - may downgrade, with a small chance to land Greater. Capped at 10 re-rolls per item.
UpgradePromotes an affix to Greater ★.
Add affix / auraAdds a fresh affix up to the tier's hard cap.
GraftMoves an affix from one item onto another.
Make IndestructibleThe item never wears out again.
CorruptSeals in a unique effect and locks the item.
Apply specificDev / tuning: apply a chosen affix or aura.
Gamble responsibly. Re-roll is a true gamble - it can make an affix worse. The 10-use limit per item means you cannot infinitely fish for a perfect roll; choose your targets.

Recycling - where reagents come from

The Cube is also a recycler. Queue a recycle bill and a Crafting colonist breaks down Rare or Legendary gear into:

  • Tier scrap - Rare and Legendary scrap, the bulk reagent for Cube operations.
  • Relic Dust - rare, from the richest items; the premium reagent (and used by Jewelry's Sanctification).
  • Tempers - single-affix reagents grafted onto gear.
Bills need the Cube. The Cube ships its own work-giver so colonists pick up recycle bills - select the Cube, add a "recycle rare / legendary gear" bill, and a Crafting colonist completes it.

Right-click & gambling

Right-click an item in Cube Gear for quick actions, and the Cube hosts a gambling reroll for players who want to chase rolls. Every numeric affix in the Cube shows its full roll range so you always know how good a roll could get.

Worked example. You have an Legendary rifle with four good affixes and one weak +1 Crafting. Recycle a stack of junk Legendaries into scrap, then graft a +Shooting Accuracy Temper over the weak affix - and upgrade your best damage affix to Greater. Two operations later it is a keeper. When you are ready to commit, corrupt it for a unique capstone - just know that locks it.
