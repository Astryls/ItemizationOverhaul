Loot Beams

A Diablo-style pillar of light over every high-tier drop - so you never miss a great item again.

The final piece of the suite plants a glowing column of light over any high-tier item the instant it hits the ground, colour-coded to its rarity. Each beam is a tapering pillar over a soft glowing pool - taller and brighter the rarer the item, with a gentle pulse and sway so it feels alive.

Top tiers only. Beams are reserved for the drops worth running for - Legendary and above. White, Magic and Rare items stay quiet so the map never turns into a light show.

Beams on the fallen

Cut down a raider carrying a tiered weapon or wearing tiered armor and the beam rises straight from the corpse. You'll spot the valuable kill clear across the battlefield long before you walk over to loot it. Corpse beams are a toggle if you'd rather keep them to dropped items only.

Hide in storage
Suppress beams on items in a stockpile or on a shelf, so organized loot stays tidy; fresh drops and battlefield loot still light up (default on).
Fade over time
Beams slowly dissipate after an item (or corpse) has sat untouched (default on, 0.25 days); hauling and re-dropping resets the timer.

Light & tidy

  • Automatic and modded-friendly. Works with every weapon and apparel the Itemization Overhaul system tiers, including modded gear - no patches needed.
  • No Harmony, no per-item overhead. Every beam is drawn from a single map component.
  • Purely cosmetic. Completely safe to add or remove mid-save; it never touches your items.
Requires Itemization Overhaul - Core. The Unique and Mythic beams light up only with the Uniques & Mythics add-on installed.
