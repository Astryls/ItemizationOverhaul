Set Bonuses & Auras

How the piece-count bonuses stack, how the capstone aura works, and what the relic boost changes.

Piece-count bonuses

Each set defines bonuses at the 2-, 3-, and 4-piece thresholds that stack cumulatively - wearing four pieces grants the 2-, 3-, and 4-piece bonuses together. The bonuses are themed to the set: armor sets give armor and pain resistance, ranged sets give accuracy and aim speed, crafter sets give work and research speed, and so on.

Internally a hidden hediff tracks how many pieces are equipped (its severity = piece count), so the bonuses update instantly as you put pieces on and take them off. The health tab shows <Set Name> (X/4).

The capstone aura

Completing the full four-piece set adds a capstone aura - a glowing disc with counter-rotating rune emblems and motes (~1.3× scale). A map component pulses it every 2.5 seconds within the aura radius:

Aura typePulse effect
NovaDeals damage to hostiles in radius (the element matches the set's theme - flame, frost, cut, blunt, burn).
WardHeals allies in radius each pulse.

A status gizmo on the wearer shows the aura icon, its effect, and its radius.

The relic boost

A set assembled from relic-rarity pieces gains +25% to every bonus. Mechanically, each 2/3/4-piece stage has a relic variant at higher severity (×1.25); when any equipped piece is relic-rarity the manager bumps the hediff into the relic stages, and the relic bump is stripped the moment the set is no longer complete. Relic stages label as X/4 ✦.

Set tooltips

Every set item's tooltip lays out the full bonus structure in a stylized block - a header, bullet bonus lines, the capstone aura, the full-set total, and a note when the relic +25% is active - so you always know exactly what completing the set will give you. Set bonuses are deliberately unpriced by Stats & Levels: the power lives in the set, not the individual piece.

Worked example - Permafrost Reaver. Two pieces give cold tolerance and blunt armor; three add move and melee; the fourth completes Frost Nova, a blunt nova that batters everything around the wearer every 2.5s. Find the four pieces at relic rarity and each number is 25% larger - a genuine winter-warlord build.
