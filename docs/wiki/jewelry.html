Jewelry & Charms

Amulets, twin rings, and inventory charms - each rolling from an exclusive affix pool, crowned by Uniques, Mythics, and the double-edged rite of Sanctification.

Three new slots

All conflict-free with your existing armor, all D2-faithful. See Slots & Charms for the full mechanics.

  • Amulet - one neck slot, invisible when worn.
  • Twin rings - two ring slots; a third is refused at equip.
  • Charms - small / large / grand talismans that work from a pawn's inventory, governed by an attunement budget.

An exclusive affix pool

Jewelry rolls 35 affixes found nowhere else in the suite - psychic boons, beast and nature effects, combat procs, defensive knots, fortune effects, and (on Desecrated pieces) eight pure curses. Rungs run Lesser → Normal → Greater → Artifact → Divine ✦. A piece carries at most 2 affixes (a third only via Sanctification).

No auras on rings & amulets. Worn jewelry never rolls a Core aura affix - not on spawn, crafting, reroll, or even corruption. Rings and amulets stay pure jewelry. Plain (un-enchanted) rings roll no jewel affixes either, honouring the drop rate.

Uniques & Mythics

A relic-tier jewelry roll can manifest as a named Unique (default 20%) - The Archon's Kaleidoscope, Corvusfrost, and more - or, rarer, a pre-sanctified Mythic with two Divine affixes and a third at Artifact. Two charm legends - The Archonexus Shard and Torch of the Burning Throne - are single-carry, with bespoke art. See the Jewelry Legends compendium for all 14 Uniques and 8 Mythics.

Sanctification & the curse

Bring Legendary-or-better jewelry to the Luminary Cube, spend a Relic Dust, and gamble: success elevates an affix to Divine; failure Desecrates the piece into a bound curse. See Sanctify & Desecrate.

Suite integration

  • Core - worn jewelry uses Core's tiers, corruption, cleansing, and faction relics.
  • Stats & Levels - worn jewelry gains Wisdom / Charisma requirements; charms gate at attunement.
  • Royalty DLC - six psychic affixes unlock with Royalty; fully playable without it.
  • Isekai - five constellation/aura affixes and gilded/blood-red aura tints.
