Relics & Faction Grudges

The rarest Relic-tier items are a faction's sacred relic - worth a fortune and +50% to all stats, but holding one turns its owners against you.

What a faction relic is

When an item rolls Relic tier, it has a chance to also be a faction relic - a named treasure "Desired by" a specific faction. A faction relic is:

  • Worth roughly ×6 market value - a fortune to sell or ransom.
  • Granted an extra +50% to all its base stats, on top of any Ethereal or affix bonuses.
  • Registered on the owning faction's ideoligion page (with Ideology), exactly like a vanilla precept relic.

The grudge

Keeping a faction's relic is an insult its owners will not forgive. While you hold it, that faction's goodwill suffers and they send grudge raids to reclaim it. You have three ways out:

ChoiceResult
Keep & fightEndure the grudge raids; the relic and its +50% are yours.
Return itHand it back for peace - and a grumbling colony that gave up a treasure.
Cleanse itCorrupt then Cleanse it into a relic of your ideology, severing the original claim.

Where relics come from

  • World rolls - any Relic-tier drop may be a faction relic.
  • Pawn gear - rare, and pawns only ever spawn carrying their own ideology's relic (never a random faction's).
  • The Relic Heist quest - an Ideology-gated questline that sends you to steal a guarded relic from another faction's shrine; the reward is the relic, and the aftermath is the grudge system.

Relic ↔ precept

Faction relics are wired into the same precept system vanilla uses for ideology relics. A faction relic claims an un-generated relic precept on its owner's ideoligion if one exists, or registers a new one (capped at five per NPC ideo to keep their pages sane). Cleansed relics move onto your ideoligion page as "Relic of [your ideology]".

Worked example - the Relic Heist. An Ideology game fires the Relic Heist quest. You raid a guarded ancient altar shrine and recover the Sunspoken Diadem, an Ancestral relic desired by the Cordis Green tribe. Equip it for +50% stats and a fortune in value - but Cordis Green is now hostile and will raid to take it back. Cleanse it, and it becomes a permanent relic of your own colony's faith.
