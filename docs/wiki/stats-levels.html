Stats & Levels

Powerful gear demands a powerful wielder. Each item is scored from its actual roll, and a pawn who does not measure up cannot equip it.

Itemization Overhaul - Stats and Levels
astryl.ItemizationOverhaulStatsAndLevels

The idea

This addon ties the loot to the Isekai RPG Leveling system: stronger gear requires a higher character level - and, for the strongest pieces, minimum stats. Loot you find too early becomes a goal to grow into rather than an instant power spike.

Requires Itemization Overhaul - Core, ISEKAI RPG LEVELING, and Harmony. The other suite addons (Runes, Uniques, Jewelry) are optional and switch on automatically when present.

What gets a requirement

ItemRequirement
White / plain gearFree - no requirement.
Rare & upLevel requirement equal to the item's Power Score, plus stat demands on the strongest rolls.
Uniques / MythicsFixed, identity-based requirements (Mythics also demand Wisdom).
RunesEach rune carries a level floor; socketed gear demands the highest one (never additive).

The Power Score

The requirement is computed deterministically from the item - nothing is saved, so it always reflects the current roll. See the Requirement Model page for the full formula. In brief: a tier base, plus per-affix and potency bonuses, plus modifiers for corruption, cleansing, and faction-relic status.

Hard-blocked, everywhere

Requirements are enforced at every equip path - float menus, dress-other, outfit stands, the caravan tab, and AI pickup - so a pawn can never wear gear they do not qualify for, with the reason spelled out on the inspect pane. Calculations are fail-open: a bug can never break equipping or rendering.

Worked example. A freshly-looted Legendary rifle with 5 affixes and a Greater roll scores, say, Level 24 and 14 Dexterity. Your level-12 shooter cannot lift it - the tooltip says so. Twelve levels of leveling later, it is exactly the upgrade you were saving it for.
