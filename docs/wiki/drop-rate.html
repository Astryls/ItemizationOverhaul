Affix Drop Rate

One slider decides what share of dropped gear spawns enchanted versus plain - and the whole suite honours that single per-item decision.

See it live. The loot roller on the home page rolls 24 drops at whatever rate you set, so you can watch the enchanted-vs-plain split shift in real time.

The setting

Under the Drop rates & tiers tab of Options → Mod settings → Itemization Overhaul - Core sits Enchanted item chance (default 100%). It is the probability that any dropped weapon or apparel spawns enchanted - rolling a tier and affixes as normal. The rest spawn plain: a true vanilla item with no tier, no affixes, no sockets, and no card.

That same tab holds the tier chance sliders (Legendary / Rare / Magic, with the leftover White % shown live), the Relic chance, the Greater-affix chance and the potency multiplier. The Affixes tab adds the Aura affix chance and the per-affix on/off config. See Loot Tiers and Crafting & Config.

Per-item roll
if random < enchantChance: roll tier + affixes + specials # enchanted else: plain White, 0 affixes, no specials # mundane

What "plain" means

A plain item is mechanically identical to a vanilla item. Because every piece of the Core's UI is gated on "does this item actually have affixes," a plain item simply renders like ordinary gear - bare name, no tier brand, no inspect card, no glow. The label and quality strip still apply, so it reads as a clean, mundane item.

On a plain itemResult
Tier banner / coloured labelnone - shows the plain item name
Affix card & tooltiphidden
Sockets, sets, uniques, jewelry affixesnone (addons skip plain items)
Level / stat requirementsnone (power is zero)

Suite-wide, from a single flag

When an item rolls plain, the Core records it and exposes IsEnchanted = false. Every addon reads that one flag, so the rate thins the entire suite uniformly:

AddonHow it honours the rate
RunesPlain items roll zero sockets.
JewelryA plain ring / amulet carries no jewel affixes.
SetsAuto-excluded - sets need Legendary+, plain stays White.
Uniques & MythicsAuto-excluded - the Unique overlay needs Legendary tier.
Stats & LevelsPlain = 0 power, so it demands nothing.
Charms stay special. Inventory charms (Jewelry addon) are dedicated rare drops and remain enchanted regardless of the rate. Crafted gear and dev/forced-rarity items are also always enchanted - the knob only thins drops.

Worked example

A lean, vanilla-leaning game. Set Enchanted item chance to 25%. Now roughly three of every four dropped weapons and apparel are plain vanilla items; the remaining quarter roll the full tier/affix/socket/set machinery. Magic loot becomes a genuine event instead of the default state - and crafting your own gear (always enchanted) becomes the reliable route to power.

Tuning advice

  • 100% (default) - every drop is enchanted; the densest ARPG experience.
  • 40 - 60% - a healthy mix; plain items are common, special finds feel earned.
  • 15 - 25% - lean and gritty; pair with raised tier odds so the rare enchanted drop is worth grabbing.
