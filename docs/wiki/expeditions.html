Expedition Chains

The only road to a Mythic: a trail of escalating ruins, each guarded harder than the last and each pointing to the next.

The trail

A chain begins with a rumor of a resting place and runs 3 to 5 legs. Each abandoned site is more fiercely guarded than the last - and each yields a star-map fragment that deciphers into the next location. At the end waits the vault, and inside it, one Mythic.

LegTypical guardians
1Bandit camps
2Fortified outposts
3Turret lines
4 - 5Sleeping mechanoids, then the vault

Reading the trail

  • Lore-driven letters arrive at every step; the trail darkens as the fragments are read.
  • Sites are marked with a violet vault icon on the world map.
  • Lose a fragment and the trail re-surfaces elsewhere; ignore it too long (60-day timeout) and it goes cold.
  • Carrying a fragment on a caravan can draw relic-hunt ambushes (~2.5-day cooldown).

Starting a chain

Chains arrive as world incidents over a long campaign. High-tier jewelry drops can also spark a chain through the addon's bridge. Dev tools (Actions → Start mythic chain…) let you trigger and step a chain for testing.

Worked example. A letter speaks of "a blade that drank a dying star." You raid the first bandit camp and recover a star-map fragment; reading it reveals a fortified outpost three tiles north. Four legs later you breach a mechanoid-guarded vault and claim The Cerulean Fury - a Mythic with the Phantom power. The chain that produced it is unique to your save's seed.

Commit to the trail. A cold or abandoned chain re-rolls its destination - you cannot bank fragments indefinitely. Run the legs while the trail is hot.
