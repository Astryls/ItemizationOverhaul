Loot Tiers

Four rarities, each with its own frame, affix budget, and spawn odds. The tier is the backbone every other system reads.

The four tiers

TierAffixesHard capSpecial ranks
White1 - 33none
Legendary2 - 44none
Ancestral3 - 55rolls Greater ★
Relicalways 77Artifact ✦ capable

The affix count is rolled within the tier's range; the hard cap can never be exceeded, even by the Luminary Cube or corruption (Relic is the exception at a fixed 7). Higher tiers also bias their stat affixes toward a curated "premium" pool of impactful stats.

Spawn odds

Each item that rolls enchanted (see Affix Drop Rate) draws a tier. The base chances are mod settings; the remainder after Legendary and Ancestral falls through to White.

Tier roll (per enchanted item)
r = random[0,1) if r < chanceAncestral -> Ancestral elif r < chanceAncestral + chanceLegendary -> Legendary else -> White relic? = (tier != White) and random < RelicChance
chanceLegendarydefault 0.12 (Scrolls & Potions raises it to 0.22)
chanceAncestraldefault 0.02 (raised to 0.06 with Scrolls & Potions)
RelicChancesmall chance that a non-White roll is promoted to Relic tier

Both chances are sliders in Options → Mod settings → Itemization Overhaul - Core. Raise them for a magic-item-rich game; lower them for a lean, vanilla-leaning feel.

The Relic tier

Relic is not a fifth slot in the roll - it is a flag set on top of an Ancestral base. A Relic item always carries 7 affixes, can roll Artifact ✦ potency, and wears a distinctive rose frame with an animated sparkle. Relics are also the only items that can become faction relics or carry corruption-only unique powers at full strength.

Worked example. An enchanted drop rolls r = 0.015, below chanceAncestral (0.02), so it is Ancestral: it gets 3-5 affixes and each has a chance to become Greater. A further RelicChance roll succeeds, promoting it to Relic - now it locks to 7 affixes, several may turn Artifact, and it could be a faction's coveted relic.

How tiers show up

  • Item card - a coloured tier banner (grey / gold / teal / rose), or "Relic" for relic-tier.
  • On the ground - the tier word replaces the vanilla quality overlay; nothing shows for plain items.
  • Glow & stars - higher tiers get a subtle coloured glow and, with Greater affixes, gold ★ stars on the label.
Tier gates the whole suite. Item sets only roll on Ancestral or Relic; uniques require Relic; level requirements scale from the tier. Lower the tier odds and the entire suite naturally produces fewer special items.
