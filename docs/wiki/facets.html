Facet Jewels

Four Rainbow-Facet gems that share the socket budget with runes - each tuned to one element, amplifying its damage and erupting on death.

The four facets

FacetElementOn-death nova
Fire facetFireFlame explosion + lingering fire
Cold facetColdFrostbite explosion
Lightning facetLightningLightning strike + EMP
Poison facetPoisonTox-gas explosion + cloud

Three effects in one gem

  • +30% damage of its element, dealt by the wearer.
  • Real armor penetration - a genuine 25% pierce of that element's resistance (not folded into the damage number).
  • 100% on-death nova - when the wearer dies, the facet erupts in its element, captured before gear strips so it always fires.

Facets are drop-only and rare (0.6%), at Unique rarity, and excluded from kill-drop rune rolls.

Synergy with damage auras

A facet does not just buff the wearer's weapon - it amplifies the matching damage aura from the Core too. The mapping:

AuraAmplified by
Holy Fire / RadianceFire facet
Holy Freeze / EntropyCold facet
Holy Shock / SuperconductorLightning facet

Socket the facet that matches your aura and its pulse damage is amplified on top of everything else.

Worked example. A corrupted relic chestplate carries the Entropy (cold) uber aura. Socket a Cold facet into a weapon on the same pawn: the wearer's cold damage rises 30% and pierces 25% of cold resistance, Entropy's chill field hits harder, and if the pawn falls, a frostbite nova erupts where they stood. A full cold build, assembled across two systems.
