Every weapon and piece of apparel becomes a loot drop with its own personality - a rarity tier, a hand of random affixes, the occasional aura, and rare twists like ethereal translucency, corruption, and consecrated relics. A full ARPG itemization layer woven over vanilla quality, plus eight addons.

The Idea

Vanilla quality is just a word on a tooltip.

Itemization Overhaul hides vanilla quality and replaces it with a Diablo-style itemization engine that rolls on top of whatever gear you have installed - vanilla, DLC, or modded. No per-item patches: every weapon and apparel automatically becomes loot with a tier, affixes, and rare modifiers.

⚔

Tiers & affixes

White, Magic, Rare, Legendary and the ultra-rare Relic - each with its own affix budget, D2/D4-style names, Greater ★ and Artifact ✦ ranks, and per-instance stat variance.

✨

Auras & corruption

Battlefield auras, ethereal and indestructible modifiers, and corruption that seals in unique named powers no roll can make.

⚗

Eight addons

Runes & gems, sets, uniques & mythics, jewelry, level requirements, scrolls & potions, endless catacombs, and rarity loot beams - all built on the same engine.

A plain drop is a mundane item - no tier and no affixes - though it still uses the mod's inspect card, shown as plain White. Every addon honours the same per-item decision, so lowering the rate thins sockets, sets, jewelry rolls and uniques too.
